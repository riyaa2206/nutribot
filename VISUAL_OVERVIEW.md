## Responsive Design

The app is designed to work on:
- **Phones**: 4.7" - 6.7" screens
- **Tablets**: 7" - 10" screens (adapts layout)
- **Orientations**: Portrait and landscape
- **Densities**: MDPI, HDPI, XHDPI, XXHDPI, XXXHDPI

All text sizes, padding, and layouts use `dp` and `sp` units for proper scaling.
